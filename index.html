How to Make Chilli

On this page I will discuss how to make chilli. Also I will include videos of other ways to make chilli.

Ingredients needed:
1 McCormick Chilli Mix(Original/Mild/Spicy)
1.5Lbs Ground Beef(80/20 mix is best)
1 Can 15oz Diced Tomatoes
1 8oz Can Tomatoe Sauce
1/8 Cup Diced Onion
1 Tbsp Minced Garlic
1 15oz Can Kidney Beans
1 15oz Can Chilli Beans
**Season to taste, I like to Use Fajita Seasoning my mom sends me and other stuff in my pantry.**
 I Start by browning the ground beef, I add a little fajita seasoning in during this process.
Once meet is 90 percent brown I start adding all the ingredients one at a time.
Cook on Medium high til it is boiling stirring occasionally.
After 5-10 Mins of boiling, lower heat to Low and cover.
Cook for another hour checking and stirring every ten minutes.
I enjoy my Chilli with Frito Chips and Mild Chedder Cheese. Enjoy!
